DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION

ARTCILE LINK DATE:12/02/2022

Digital transformation involves using digital technologies to change a business process to become more efficient or effective. The idea is to use technology not just to replicate an existing service in a digital form,but to use technology to transform that service into something significantly better. It sounds simple but digital transformation can be a long, expensive and complicated process that doesn't always go according to plan.
